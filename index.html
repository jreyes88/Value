For every $100 of federal spending, public broadcasting receives just a penny .

President Trump’s budget proposal threatens federal funding for the Corporation for Public Broadcasting which funds the nation’s public broadcasting system including KLRU-TV, Austin PBS.

What does this mean for KLRU?

  • Loss of $1.7 million in annual revenue (or 12% of our budget).
  • Increased production costs that would impinge our ability to create local programs like Austin City Limits, Overheard with Evan Smith, and Central Texas Gardener.
  • Loss of funding to support KLRU’s extensive educational outreach.
  • Potential loss of national programming such as Independent Lens, NOVA, and Frontline to name a few.
  • Increased costs for station to operate.
  • Loss of independent journalism.
